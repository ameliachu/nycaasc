Track 3: Beyond


Susie Kim


Susie Kim is a woman of color educator invested in liberatory consciousness. As a 2013 NYC Teach For America Corps Member, she currently teaches 7th grade history and writing in East New York, Brooklyn. Susie attended UC San Diego, where she double majored in Ethnic Studies and International Studies. She is passionate about working with youth to develop their critical consciousness and academic skills so that they can advocate for themselves and their communities.
