Deshaun Mars


Deshaun Mars is a Director of Partnerships at Teach For America. In this role, Deshaun works with a large portfolio of school principals and leaders in New York City to help facilitate the hiring process for the New York region's incoming corps members. In addition, Deshaun also manages the recruitment efforts of the New York region to help diversify Teach For America's local teaching corps. A graduate of Brown University, where he earned both his B.A. and M.A. degrees in Education Studies and Urban Education Policy, respectively, Deshaun started his career as a college access counselor for the Cypress Hills Local Development Corporation, a comprehensive community development and social service organization serving residents in the Cypress Hills/East New York section of Brooklyn. There, he mentored and helped students apply to college. He then worked as a college transition counselor with Good Shepherd Services, assisting the agency's high school graduates transition to community college at the City University of New York. Deshaun is a proud New Yorker, born and raised in Brooklyn.
