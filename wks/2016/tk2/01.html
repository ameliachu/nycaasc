Track 2: Around

How has White Supremacy Shaped the Foods We Eat?


Description

Who decides what food you eat? How do you decide what foods to eat? How is the food you eat indicative of local and global corporate, colonial, and class structures? In this workshop, we will use food as an access point to discuss larger structural issues such as sustainability, white supremacy, and colonialism.
