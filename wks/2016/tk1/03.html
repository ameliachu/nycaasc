Track 1: Within

Domestic Violence 101 for Millennials


Description

Facilitated by Heather Schultz, (communications and development manager at The Donaldson Adoption Institute and domestic violence survivor) and Flora Ichiou Huang, (child welfare liaison and current program coordinator for the Lower East Side Community Partnership Program), the "Domestic Violence 101 for Millennials" workshop will review the three-part cycle of domestic violence coined by psychologist Lenore E. Walker in 1979, the profile of an abuser, and domestic violence detection and prevention. The workshop will also explore the impact of social media on domestic violence awareness and dismantle stereotypes of battered Asian women.
