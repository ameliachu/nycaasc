Track 3: Beyond

Far Beyond the Stars


Description

This workshop aims to establish the necessary connection between the radical imagination and activism. Through our creative energies and efforts we imagine a different and often better world. To do this is to be radical. How, then, do our radical imaginations lead to a different type of worldbuilding--one that takes place in our reality?

We invite conference attendees to bring in their creative work and share with Aatmaja, Arielle, Kris ,Preeti, Ryan, and Wendy. All formats welcome, from poetry to comics to fiction/non-fiction
